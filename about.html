We Are The Revivalists

Revival House Global is a divine movement called to reveal Jesus to humanity across all nations. We are a global family, chosen by God to disciple billions through the ministry of His Word and power. Christ is before us, behind us, beside us, and within us. We are revivalists - carriers of God's fire, rejoicing in the Spirit, and walking in favor everywhere we go.

Our Vision.

To reveal Jesus Christ to every nation, tribe and tongue through the uncompromising preaching of the Gospel, demonstration of God's power, and discipleship of believers into their divine purpose. We envision a global network of revival hubs where God's presence transforms lives and communities.

Our Mandate.

We are called to raise and send forth revivalists who will disciple nations through:

  • Powerful Word ministry
  • Supernatural demonstrations
  • Strategic prayer initiatives
  • Comprehensive discipleship systems
  • Global missionary outreach

"Christ before us, Christ behind us, Christ beside us, Christ within us. We are a move of God's Spirit that cannot be stopped - called to ignite nations with the fire of revival."

Revival House Global Declaration

Our Core Values

Christ-Centeredness

We keep Jesus as the absolute focus of all we do - His person, His teachings, and His finished work. Everything emanates from intimacy with Christ and leads back to glorifying Him.

Bible-Based Foundation

We build everything on the infallible Word of God. Scripture is our final authority for doctrine, practice, and daily living. We are committed to teaching the whole counsel of God.

Spirit-Empowered Living

We pursue and depend on the Holy Spirit's power for life and ministry. The gifts and fruit of the Spirit are essential for fulfilling our divine assignment.

Global Discipleship

We are committed to making disciples (not just converts) across all nations - teaching believers to obey everything Christ commanded until they become reproducers of the faith.


What We Believe.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God - our complete and final authority for faith and practice (2 Timothy 3:16-17).

The Godhead

We believe in one God eternally existing in three Persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit - equal in essence while distinct in function (Matthew 28:19)

Salvation

We believe salvation comes only through faith in Jesus Christ - His death, burial and resurrection. This salvation brings redemption, healing and deliverance (Acts 4:12).

The Church

We believe the Church is God's end-time agent for kingdom advancement - called to preach the Gospel, make disciples, and demonstrate God's power (Matthew 16:18-19).
