Shape Your Life with the Words of Life

This transformative series will teach you how to wield God's Word as the ultimate tool for personal transformation. Through practical teachings and spiritual exercises, you'll learn how to:

  • Renew your mind with Scripture
  • Speak life over your circumstances
  • Stand on God's promises in every area of life
  • Develop a lifestyle of Word meditation
  • See tangible results from applying God's Word

Join us every Sunday in September as we explore different dimensions of living by the Word. Each session will include teaching, practical application, and ministry time.

  • Speaker: Apostle Mike Ajie & Ministry Team
  • Date: Every Sunday in September 2025
  • Time: During Main Services (8:00AM & 10:30AM)
  • Location: Revival House Global HQ, Ikorodu, Lagos
  • Tag: #WordPoweredLife
