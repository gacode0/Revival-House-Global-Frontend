Join the Revival Army

At Revival House Global, volunteering is more than service - it's a kingdom assignment. God is calling laborers into His harvest, and your unique gifts are needed to amplify this global revival. As part of our team, you'll grow spiritually while helping reveal Jesus to humanity across nations.

We believe every member is called to minister. Whether through prayer, media, outreach or logistics, your participation helps advance God's kingdom. Our volunteers experience supernatural growth as they serve, with many discovering their divine purpose through their service.


Serve Through These Ministry Teams

Ushering & Protocol

Create an atmosphere of excellence by welcoming people into God's presence. This team ensures smooth service operations while demonstrating Christ's love through hospitality and order. You'll help first-time visitors feel at home while maintaining a worshipful environment.

NextGen Ministry.

Shape young lives for Christ in our children and youth ministries. Through creative teaching, worship, and activities, you'll help disciple the next generation of revivalists. Training provided for all children's worker

Music Team.

Help facilitate supernatural encounters through music, dance, and creative expressions. Whether vocal, instrumental, technical, or visual arts, your gifts will usher people into God's presence. Auditions required for some roles.

Prayer Team/Intercession Team.

Be on the frontlines of spiritual warfare through prayer. This team covers all services, events, and leadership in prayer. Members receive training in prophetic intercession and spiritual discernment.

Administrative Support.

Support ministry operations through organizational and clerical skills. From data management to guest services, your contribution keeps the ministry running efficiently behind the scenes.

Media & Production.

Help spread the revival through audio, video, lighting, and online platforms. No experience needed for some roles - we provide training. Technical minds and creative thinkers are especially needed.

Technical, I.C.T & Stage Management

Ensure our conferences and outreaches run smoothly through logistics, setup, and coordination. This team is crucial for creating environments where miracles happen.

Outreach & Evangelism

Take revival to the streets through local missions and evangelism initiatives. Team members receive training in soul-winning, deliverance ministry, and practical outreach strategies.
